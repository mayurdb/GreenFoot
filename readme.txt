Frog dash - 
It is an arcade game about a funny and cheerful frog who skates, jumps and flys. You have to eat the insects on the way to collect points. But be careful and stay away from the snakes. Game also features "Helicopter" power-up which enables the player to fly by opening a bonus session. Player also gets a chance to take extra lifes on the way.  


INSTRUCTIONS-
1. "space" - JUMP
2. "up-arrow" - MOVE UP
3. "down-arrow" - MOVE DOWN
